# Planning Metrics

Business planning review uses these decision inputs:

- `forecast_gap_pct`: absolute gap between current forecast and operating plan.
- `board_deadline_hours`: time left before board or executive review materials must be locked.
- `cross_team_dependency`: number of teams needed to resolve the assumption.
- `exec_visibility`: whether the item is likely to appear in CEO/COO or board discussion.
- `revenue_impact`: relative ARR or bookings impact on a 1-5 scale.

The first action is not always the largest gap. A smaller gap can be first when it blocks the board narrative, needs cross-team alignment, or carries high revenue impact.
