# Weekly Review Log

## Monday 08:30

- Sales reports a pull-in risk on a strategic renewal.
- Finance needs the board reforecast narrative by Tuesday morning.
- Marketing asks whether a segment naming change affects the pipeline dashboard, but no executive decision is blocked.
- CS flags a long-tail churn variance that is large but does not change this week's operating decision.

## Decision Rule

The planning team should choose the first review item by combining forecast gap, meeting deadline, cross-team dependency, executive visibility, and revenue impact.
